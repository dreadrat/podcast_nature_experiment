Perceived Restorativeness Scale (PRS11)

The Perceived Restorativeness Scale (PRS11) is used to measure how restorative an environment is. The scale includes various aspects such as being away, fascination, coherence, and scope. Each aspect is rated, and higher scores indicate a more restorative environment.
